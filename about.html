gnietz. About Me

Welcome to Gnietz! I’m Gürkan, a dedicated web designer and digital marketing enthusiast living in the vibrant city of Antalya. My passion for technology and creativity drives me to explore the ever-evolving digital landscape, where I strive to make a meaningful impact.

Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in Computer Programming at Akdeniz University. This academic journey not only equips me with essential technical skills but also fuels my desire to specialize in digital marketing. I believe that a strong online presence is crucial for businesses today, and I am eager to learn how to leverage digital tools to help them succeed.

My primary focus is on developing innovative projects in the health tourism sector. This area fascinates me because it combines my interests in technology and wellness, allowing me to create solutions that benefit both providers and clients. I aim to design websites that not only look great but also provide an exceptional user experience, ultimately driving engagement and conversion.

Thank you for visiting Gnietz! Feel free to reach out with any questions or collaboration ideas—I’d love to connect!
